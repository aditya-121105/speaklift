# SpeakLift: User Journeys

User Journeys describe the overarching emotional and functional arc a user takes through the platform over days or weeks.

## 1. The "Placement Preparation" Journey (New Student)
- **Motivation**: Placements begin in 3 weeks. They are terrified of technical interviews.
- **Pain Points**: They don't know what companies will ask. They ramble when nervous.
- **The Journey**:
  1. Signs up. Uploads their basic resume.
  2. Creates a mock interview by pasting a generic "Software Engineer Trainee" JD.
  3. Takes the interview. Fumbles a question about API design.
  4. The AI Report gently points out their use of filler words and recommends a specific framework (STAR method) for the API question.
  5. The AI adds "API Design Basics" to their Weekly Roadmap.
  6. They return daily to clear their roadmap tasks, watching their Readiness Score climb from 40% to 75%.
- **Success Metric**: Readiness score exceeds 80%.

## 2. The "High Achiever Optimization" Journey
- **Motivation**: Has an onsite loop with a Tier-1 tech company next week.
- **Pain Points**: Needs to optimize vocabulary and semantic density. Wants to sound like a Senior Engineer, not just capable.
- **The Journey**:
  1. Skips standard onboarding. Pastes a highly specific Senior Staff JD into the Interview Setup.
  2. Breezes through the interview.
  3. Dives deep into the AI Report. Ignores the "Good job" praise and drills into the "Lexical Density" and "Concept Coverage" metrics.
  4. The AI Insight notes that while technically correct, their tone was hesitant (high hedging phrases).
  5. They immediately trigger a specialized "Communication Only" practice run.
- **Success Metric**: Zero hedging phrases detected. 95% Concept Coverage.

## 3. The "Career Switcher" Journey
- **Motivation**: Transitioning from Marketing to Data Analysis. 
- **Pain Points**: Lacks confidence using technical jargon naturally.
- **The Journey**:
  1. Uploads a resume that is heavy on marketing.
  2. The AI identifies the gap and builds a "Vocabulary Bridge" roadmap.
  3. The daily insights focus specifically on incorporating SQL and Python terminology naturally into behavioral answers.
- **Success Metric**: Vocabulary richness score increases; AI stops flagging "layman terms".
