# SpeakLift: Dashboard Philosophy

## The AI Career Command Center
The dashboard is the user's home. It must not feel like a static grid of widgets. Instead, it should feel like walking into a high-tech locker room where their AI coach is waiting for them. It is dynamic, personalized, and actionable.

### 1. The Hero Section: The Greeting & The Insight
- **Dynamic Greeting**: "Good evening, Sarah. You're 3 days away from your Google mock interview."
- **Today's AI Insight**: Immediately below the greeting, the AI surfaces a single, highly relevant piece of advice based on their last session. Example: *"I noticed you're still relying heavily on the word 'basically'. Let's focus on pausing instead of using filler words today."*
- **Primary Action**: A massive, beautifully styled "Start Practice" button. It should glow or pulse subtly to draw the eye.

### 2. The Readiness Score
- A prominent, beautiful, circular progress visual or a sleek dial representing their overarching **Interview Readiness Score**.
- It is the north star metric. The UI should make it feel like a credit score—something to be nurtured and protected.
- Hovering or tapping reveals the breakdown (Technical: 85%, Communication: 72%, Confidence: 90%).

### 3. Upcoming Interviews
- A horizontal scroll or a sleek card list of target companies/roles they are preparing for.
- Displays the Job Title, Company (if applicable), and a mini readiness gauge specific to that JD.

### 4. Skill Progress & Interview Timeline
- **The Graph**: A stunning, minimalist line chart showing their Readiness Score progression over the last 30 days. No heavy grid lines, just a smooth curve with a soft gradient fill below it.
- **The Timeline**: A GitHub-style contribution graph (the "commit grid") showing days they practiced. This drives the streak mechanic visually.

### 5. Quick Actions (Command Center)
A row of beautifully designed, glassmorphic cards offering immediate utility:
- Upload New Resume
- Analyze Job Description
- Practice Specific Topic (e.g., "System Design")
- View Full Learning Roadmap

### 6. Recent Reports & Feedback
- A clean, feed-like list of recent interview sessions.
- Displays the date, the overall score, and a quick one-sentence AI summary of the performance.
- Clicking expands into the full Interview Intelligence Report (M3.2).

### 7. Achievements & Milestones
- Tasteful, muted visual badges celebrating milestones (e.g., "10-Day Streak", "Vocabulary Master", "Zero Filler Words").
- These should feel like prestigious awards, not mobile game loot.

### 8. The Learning Roadmap
- Directly pulled from the M3.2 Intelligence Engine.
- A clear, bulleted list of 3 things they need to study or practice *this week*.
- Example: "Review ACID properties" or "Practice answering 'Tell me about a time you failed'."

## Personalization Rule
If the dashboard looks exactly the same on Tuesday as it did on Monday, we have failed. The content (insights, roadmap, score, timeline) must reflect the user's immediate context and recent actions.
