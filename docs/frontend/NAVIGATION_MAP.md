# SpeakLift: Navigation Map

A strict visual outline of the routing hierarchy.

```text
/ (Marketing Home)
│
├── /login
├── /register
│
├── /dashboard (Authenticated Home)
│   ├── Readiness Score Widget
│   ├── AI Insight Widget
│   └── Quick Actions
│
├── /interviews
│   ├── /interviews/new (Setup Wizard: JD / Resume selection)
│   ├── /interviews/[id]/execute (The Active Mock Interview)
│   └── /interviews/[id]/report (The Intelligence Report)
│
├── /roadmap
│   ├── Weekly Goals
│   └── Skill Progression Analytics
│
├── /profile
│   ├── Target Jobs
│   ├── Resume Management
│   └── Skill Matrix
│
└── /settings
    ├── Account
    ├── Preferences (Theme, Notifications)
    └── Subscription (Future)
```
